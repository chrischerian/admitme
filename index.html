What We Do

AdmitMe offers a many different areas of personal success and growth. We know that different students need help with different aspects of their college portfoliio. As such, we've created different packages to cater to these unique needs.

Extracurricular Education

Extracurriculars are a not only heavily evaluated in a college admissions portfolio, they're also critical to a student's growth and education. We'll enable your student's passions and interests!

Standardized Testing

Tests like the SAT and ACT may seem frustrating, confusing, and overwhelming, but we're here to help your students not only survive these exams, but soar above expectations.

College Essays

The prospect of writing multiple college essays is sometimes daunting. More than just editing for grammar, we aim to help craft your student's narrative to put their best self forward.

Academic Acheivement

Grades are a core part of any student's admissions portfolio. By understanding a student's habits and working styles, we can develop a plan to help them unlock their potential.

Awards and Excellence

Pursuing interests and enjoying high school are important, but its also vital that these passions are demonstrated tangibly. We'll advise you and your students on what awards and acheivements to aim for.

Professional Development

Life doesn't end with high school or college. Our materials are meant to prepare students for the long road ahead by assisting with professional skills they can carry with them for life.
